Join Us

Our team is driven by a single mission: To advance the efficiency frontier of robust & safe physical AI through fully open and reproducible research. If you are excited about physical AI, world models and autonomy, and thrilled to join us on our mission, apply for one of the following roles:

AI Research Scientist

Drive frontier AI research as an AI research scientist. Lead your own research aligned with KE:SAI's core mission, publish reproducible work with open models and code, and contribute to a culture where scientific rigour and openness go hand in hand. You will have the opportunity to mentor interns and PhD students.

AI Research Engineer

Support frontier AI research as an AI research engineer. You will own the infrastructure that makes science move fast, from scalable training pipelines and rigorous experiment tracking to the kind of hyperparameter intuition and systems reliability that researchers rarely have time to develop but deeply depend on.

PhD Student

Pursue a PhD at the cutting edge of physical AI, supervised by world-leading researchers. You will be embedded in a collaborative environment, with access to large-scale compute and real-world data, and are expected to publish at top-tier conferences.

Intern

Join us for a research internship and work directly on open problems in world models, computer vision and autonomous driving. We welcome highly motivated undergraduate, graduate and PhD students with prior research experience for stays of four months or longer.

Your Profile

You have great analytical skills, are highly-motivated, curious, creative and a good team player. You have a Master's degree (or equivalent) in computer science, mathematics, physics or a closely related field. You have a very good mathematical understanding, great coding skills and prior research experience related to KE:SAI's mission, in particular in areas related to computer vision, robotics and machine learning. If you apply for a Technical Staff position, you have earned a PhD and authored papers at top-tier conferences in computer vision, robotics or machine learning. You are excited about KE:SAI's mission and committed to open science. You are looking to work with some of the most talented researchers in the field at the frontier of AI. This is you? Then join us on our mission!

Your Application

To apply, send your application package as one single pdf with maximally 10 pages to: talent@kesai.eu. Your application ideally includes the following information in this order:

  • A Cover Letter in which you state (i) why you want to work for KE:SAI and (ii) your most significant accomplishment. We are particularly interested in the how: the steps you took, and what you learned.
  • Your Curriculum Vitae (CV) including links to your Google Scholar, Github and social media profiles as well as a list of reference letter writes that know you and your research and can be contacted by us.
  • Your Research Statement describing your prior research as well as your future research agenda. Include links to research papers (Technical Staff) and/or your Bachelor / Master thesis (PhD Student, Intern).
  • Your University Transcripts, for both undergraduate as well as graduate level studies (if applicable).
