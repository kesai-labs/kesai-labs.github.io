2. What data we process

When you visit this website, our web server automatically records the following data in server log files: your IP address, date and time of the request, the requested URL, the referring URL (if any), the HTTP status code, and the browser and operating system you are using. This data is technically necessary to deliver the website and is not linked to any personal identity.

If you contact us by e-mail, we process the personal data you provide (name, e-mail address, message content) solely for the purpose of handling your enquiry.

3. Legal basis

Server log data is processed on the basis of Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in operating a secure and functional website). E-mail enquiries are processed on the basis of Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (pre-contractual or contractual measures) or Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in responding to your enquiry).

4. Retention periods

Server log files are deleted after 14 days at the latest. E-mail correspondence is kept for as long as necessary to handle your enquiry and for up to 3 years thereafter for documentation purposes, unless statutory retention obligations apply.

5. Third parties and data transfers

This website does not use any analytics services, tracking tools, advertising networks, or social media plugins. All fonts and scripts are self-hosted; no third-party requests are made when you visit this site. We do not transfer your data to third parties unless we are legally required to do so.

6. Cookies and local storage

This website does not use cookies. We use the browser's sessionStorage API solely to remember your scroll position within your current browsing session. This data is never transmitted to our servers and is automatically deleted when you close the browser tab.

For Germany, the relevant authority is the Landesbeauftragte für den Datenschutz und die Informationsfreiheit Baden-Württemberg.
